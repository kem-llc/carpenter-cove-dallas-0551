Our story

Crafting Quality Since 2009

To provide Dallas homeowners and businesses with exceptional carpentry services that combine traditional craftsmanship with modern techniques.

Carpenter Cove was founded in 2009 by master carpenter John Harris, who saw a need for high-quality, custom woodwork in the Dallas area. Starting with a small workshop and a handful of tools, John built a reputation for precision and reliability. Over the years, the team grew as word spread about their meticulous attention to detail and commitment to customer satisfaction.

Today, Carpenter Cove serves clients across Dallas, from historic homes in Highland Park to modern lofts in Deep Ellum. Every project, whether a simple repair or a full renovation, receives the same dedication to excellence. We believe that great carpentry is an art form, and we take pride in creating pieces that enhance your home for years to come.
