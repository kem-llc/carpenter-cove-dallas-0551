About Carpenter Cove

To deliver exceptional carpentry that enhances the beauty and function of every space we touch.

Carpenter Cove was founded in Dallas with a passion for woodworking and a commitment to quality. Our founder, a master carpenter with over 15 years of experience, started the company to bring custom craftsmanship to homeowners and businesses in the area. From the beginning, we focused on precision, integrity, and personalized service.

Today, we are a team of skilled artisans who take pride in every project, big or small. We believe that great carpentry transforms a house into a home, and we strive to exceed expectations with every cut, joint, and finish.
